My Egusi soup Recipe

Description

Simple recipe for delicious Egusi soup to enjoy with Eba or Pounded Yam.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Grinded egusi
  • Palm oil
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Onions
  • Salt
  • Green leaves
  • Seasoning cubes

Steps

  1. Heat palm oil in pot.
  2. Pour in sliced onions and pepper to fry in palm oil for 2 mins.
  3. Pour in grinded egusi and fry for a min while stiring aggressively.
  4. Pour in water and stir.
  5. Leave it till it starts to simmer.
  6. Add salt, crayfish and seasoning cubes to taste.
  7. Cook till its almost done then add the chopped leaves, let it cook for an extra 2 mins.
  8. Server egusi with eba, pounded yam or fufu.
