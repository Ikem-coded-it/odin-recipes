My Stew Recipe

Description

Simple recipe for delicious Stew to enjoy with rice or yam.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Fresh Tomatoes
  • Tin tomatoe
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Vegetable oil.
  • Salt.
  • Onions.
  • Seasoning cubes.

Steps

  1. Heat vegetable oil in pot.
  2. Pour in sliced onions and pepper then fry till they turn white.
  3. Add in tin tomatoe and fry along with the onions for like 5 mins.
  4. Pour in the blended fresh tomatoes and keep frying for extra 5 mins.
  5. Add water so it starts boiling.
  6. Add salt, seasoning and crayfish to taste.
  7. Keep boiling until it's done.
  8. Serve stew with rice or yam.
