My Indomie Recipe

Description

Simple recipe for delicious indomie.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Indomie
  • Eggs
  • Pepper
  • Crayfish
  • Onions, Carrots, Green beans.

Steps

  1. Boil 60cl of water.
  2. Add in packet of indomie and all its contents.
  3. Add sliced carrots, onions and green beans.
  4. Add crayfish and pepper.
  5. Cook on low heat till it's almost dry.
  6. Crack open two eggs, add a pinch of salt and crayfish to taste then stir.
  7. Fry eggs in oil.
  8. Serve Indomie with fried eggs.
